UFAZ represented at the International Education Fair in Ashgabat

On November 14-15, 2019, UFAZ participated in the annual International Education Exhibition held in Ashgabat, Turkmenistan, at the invitation of the Ministry of Education of Turkmenistan, UFAZ Secretary General Charlotte Payen participated in the international education fair and the scientific conference organized on a annual basis by the Turkmen government.

Following a traditional opening ceremony and senior government officials’ visit to the exhibitors’ booths that UFAZ shared with the French Institute of Ashgabat, UFAZ Secretary General was given to opportunity to briefly present the French-Azerbaijani University to the Vice Premier Mr Agamyradov in presence of the French Ambassador of France to Turkmenistan.

Research trip to AzLab for UFAZ fourth-year Oil and Gas Engineering students

On November 12, 2019, fourth-year Oil and Gas Engineering students visited “AZLAB” Exploration and Research Center, one of the largest research centers in the region together with UFAZ Student Affairs Specialist Gunay Farzaliyeva. QHSE Manager Seymur Davidov welcomed students and provided them with the safety instructions before visiting the laboratories.

Students also visited Gas Chromatography Room and Oil Analysis Lab, accompanied by Analytics Lab Manager Yevgeniy Juravlyov, and received insights about the process.

Ernst and Young (EY) representatives delivered a lecture to UFAZ students

On November 25, 2019, EY Climate Change and Sustainability Services Senior Farah Mammadli and Staff Assistant Sheida Karimova delivered a lecture to UFAZ fourth-year Geosciences Engineering and Computer science students on sustainable development in ecological and economic perspectives.

Mrs. Mammadli also provided insights on evaluation criteria for social responsibility evolution in society, ecological balance of natural resources extraction and sustainable operation that are factors affecting economic development. To illustrate linear economy limitations and benefits of the circular economy, she provided global statistical data.

UFAZ participated in SPE Annual Caspian Technical Conference 2019

On October 18, 2019, UFAZ Secretary-General Charlotte Payen represented UFAZ at the 6th annual SPE Annual Caspian Technical Conference focused on the digital transformation taking place in the oil and gas industry.

This panel involved several other speakers, including, BP HR Manager for GPO, RD and Strategy & Region Mehriban Ayyubova, Schlumberger HR Director to Workforce Planning and Talent Management Fabrice Bouchard, Baker Hughes ASPIRE Services Operation Emilya Mammadova.

UFAZ Geosciences Engineering students on a site visit to Baker Hughes Company

On November 5, 2019 UFAZ fourth-year Geoscience Engineering students, together with UFAZ Oil and Gas Coordinator Fidan Aslanzade, visited Baker Hughes, Wireline logging site. Baker Hughes is an energy technology company that provides solutions for energy and industrial customers worldwide. Baker Hughes Geoscientist Aleksandra Khramtseva, welcomed UFAZ students and provided them with safety instruction before visiting the laboratories.

Students were accompanied on site by Mr Nusret Imamverdiyev, Staff Development Manager, to observe the operational capacity and processing in three laboratories namely, hydraulic laboratory, mechanical laboratory and electricity laboratory.
